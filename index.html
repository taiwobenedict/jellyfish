About JellyFish

JellyFish is a groundbreaking mobile virtual network operator (MVNO) that seamlessly integrates web3 technologies with mobile connectivity. Our mission is to revolutionize the way you experience the internet and access online services.

With JellyFish, you can unlock the full potential of web3 on your mobile device. We bridge the gap between traditional telecom networks and the web3 platform, offering a comprehensive web3 mobile experience. Connect to decentralized finance (DeFi) applications, deploy smart contracts, and access decentralized applications (DApps) for seamless automation. We prioritize security and privacy, employing advanced encryption, secure boot processes, and cold wallet isolation. Join us and embark on a transformative mobile journey empowered by web3 technologies.

Features of
JellyFish Mobile

  • AES128 Encryption: Your data is protected with state-of-the-art encryption technology.
  • RSA & ECC: Enjoy secure digital signatures and a robust handshake between your mobile device and the network.
  • Remote Management (OTA): Receive security patches and policy enforcement updates remotely for optimal protection.
  • Secure Boot Process: Ensure the integrity of the software running on your device, preventing unauthorized modifications.
  • Cold Wallet Isolation: Safeguard your funds by isolating the cold wallet from your phone's operating system.
  • SIM Swap Prevention: Your SIM can only be recovered through seed keys generated upon SIM activation.
  • Cold Wallet Address Obfuscation: Enhance your privacy with obfuscated cold wallet addresses.
